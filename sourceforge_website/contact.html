Contact

There are various ways to get in touch with the developers and other players.

Join our Discord server to chat with developers and other players, get help, and discuss all things Dune Legacy. The most active community platform for Dune Legacy: https://discord.com/invite/6sAcZr6y3B

Forum

You may use our forum to get help, make suggestions and discuss Dune Legacy in general.

Reporting Bugs

If you found a bug you may decide to report it.

Feature Requests

If you have a concrete suggestion for an improvement of Dune Legacy you may open a feature request.
