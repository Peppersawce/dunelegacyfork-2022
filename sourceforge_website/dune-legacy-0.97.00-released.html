Dune Legacy 0.97.00 released

Dec 27, 2022

After a 6 year delay, the next version of Dune Legacy is ready for download. I'll be doing some additional patches soon to add features

Changes:

  • Fixed ornithopters to fly properly again, there's still a few bugs to make them work properly
  • New reworked campaign scenario maps by Dimitri, provide feedback to dmitrif AT bk.ru
  • Tweaks to quantbot, such as considering max units
  • A new campaign AI
  • Cleanup of SDL2 libraries and library references
  • in keeping with original game, units only return to original repair locaiton if 'manual carryall drop' is enabled. This is similar to original and also - returning to battle zone is a bit imbalanced - allows for snowballing
