Dune Legacy 0.98.1 Released

May 1, 2025

I am excited to announce the release of Dune Legacy version 0.98.1 for Windows and macOS! This update brings major performance improvements and important bug fixes.

Key Improvements in 0.98.1

  • Major performance improvements: much less laggy graphics and much less slow down with large numbers of units!
  • Fix manual carryall drop bug (especially with repair yards)
  • Some AI tweaks
  • Additional fixes by Henric Jungheim

Download

  • Windows: Dune Legacy 0.98.1-vs-win64 Setup.exe
  • macOS: DuneLegacy-0.98.1-macOS.dmg
